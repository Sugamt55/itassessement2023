Working with us

Design Group is team of architects, landscape architects, engineers, builders, furniture designers, writers, film makers, and researchers representing 20 countries across the globe. Our diverse staff collectively enables a dynamic work environment, and we’re always learning from each other. We want to bring the best solutions to some of the world’s most pressing challenges. Design Group offers competitive compensation and many members of our team have the opportunity to travel. Staff should be eager to spend months at a time in the field immersed in the projects and communities with whom we work.
